About IIPS

International Indian Public School (IIPS) is an educational institution that offers a world-class learning environment for students from all walks of life. With a commitment to academic excellence and holistic development, IIPS strives to empower students to become global citizens while retaining strong ties to their cultural roots.

Located in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, IIPS is a hub for learning, providing quality education to students from kindergarten through to senior secondary levels. Our dynamic teaching methods focus on the overall development of students, including academic, athletic, and extracurricular achievements.

Academics at IIPS

IIPS follows a curriculum designed to meet international standards while adhering to the best practices in the Indian education system. Our academic programs include:

  • Pre-Primary Education
  • Primary & Secondary Education
  • Senior Secondary Education (Grades 11 & 12)
  • Extracurricular and Co-curricular Activities
  • Sports and Physical Education

Our teachers are highly qualified and dedicated to creating a stimulating and supportive learning environment for students to excel in all areas of life.

Admissions

We welcome students from various backgrounds and cultures. The admission process at IIPS is designed to ensure a seamless experience for both students and parents. The following documents are required for the admission process:

  • Completed Admission Form
  • Previous Academic Records
  • Passport Copy
  • Birth Certificate
  • Recent Passport-sized Photograph

If you are interested in applying or would like more information, please contact our admissions office.
